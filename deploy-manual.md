1. Go to https://netlify.com
2. Sign up for free
3. Drag `rma-frontend.zip` to deploy
4. Get instant URL

### Option 2: Vercel  
1. Go to https://vercel.com
2. Sign up for free
3. Upload `dist/` folder
4. Get instant URL
